Mild Symptoms

With mild COVID-19:


* You may have a fever, including one that doesn't reach the 37.8°C mark.
* You may lose your sense of smell or taste.
* You may have tiredness, muscles aches or a headache.
* You are not highly likely to have sore throat or runny nose, but they do occur in some cases.
* You do not have marked breathlessness.

Severe Symptoms

With Severe COVID-19:


* You may have high fever above 103℉ (39.4℃)
* You may have difficulty breathing
* You may have blue lips or face
* You may have persistent pain or pressure in the chest
* You may have new-onset confusion (“altered mental status”)
* You may have trouble waking up, even when another person tries to wake them
